# ET AI Hackathon 2026: Curated Scope

## Why this scope

This project intentionally focuses on one primary statement with two supporting statements, because depth and working automation score better than trying to cover all tracks.

## Primary Statement

- Problem Statement 8: AI-Native News Experience

Implemented modules:

- News Navigator: Interactive intelligence briefing with follow-up Q&A.
- Story Arc Tracker: Timeline, entities, sentiment, and what-to-watch context.
- AI Video Studio: AI-generated 60-120s storyboard package with narration and web-backed source context.
- Vernacular Engine: Hindi and Telugu summaries in the same workflow.

## Supporting Statements

- Problem Statement 1: AI for Enterprise Content Operations
- Problem Statement 2: Agentic AI for Autonomous Enterprise Workflows

Implemented depth:

- Multi-agent stage flow: Ingestion -> Drafting -> Compliance -> Editor -> Approval.
- Compliance-aware pipeline with confidence and risk telemetry.
- Auditable trail of agent actions and system decisions.
- Fallback handling when provider or connectivity degrades.

## What was intentionally not included

To keep the submission coherent and demo-ready, these were excluded:

- CRM-native sales operations workflows (PS4).
- Personal finance planning modules (PS9).
- Domain-specific regulated workflows (PS5).
- Enterprise cost optimization actions (PS3).

## 3-minute demo flow

1. Paste article text or URL and run live analysis.
2. Show pipeline movement across all agent stages.
3. Ask a follow-up question in News Navigator.
4. Open Story Arc Tracker and sentiment/entity outputs.
5. Generate AI video brief and open Studio player.
6. Show provider transparency and audit trail.
