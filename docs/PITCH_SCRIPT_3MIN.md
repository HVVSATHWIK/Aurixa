# 3-Minute Pitch Script

## 0:00 - 0:30 Problem

Teams in business-news operations still spend too much time moving from raw article input to compliant, localized, multi-format output. The process is slow, inconsistent, and hard to audit.

## 0:30 - 1:10 Solution

AURIXA is a multi-agent runtime that executes a full cycle:

- Ingestion
- Drafting
- Compliance scoring
- Editor corrections
- Approval

Then it automatically powers:

- interactive briefing Q&A,
- story arc tracking,
- vernacular summaries,
- and AI video brief generation with online source context.

## 1:10 - 2:20 Live Demo Sequence

1. Submit article text.
2. Show pipeline progressing node by node.
3. Open News Navigator and ask one follow-up question.
4. Show Story Arc timeline and Opportunity Radar signals.
5. Build video brief and open Studio player.
6. Point to provider transparency and audit trail entries.

## 2:20 - 2:50 Impact

Using the model in docs/IMPACT_MODEL.md:

- Estimated cycle-time reduction: about 71 percent.
- Estimated daily capacity gain for 30 cycles: about 27 team-hours.
- Added governance via confidence/risk telemetry and decision audit logs.

## 2:50 - 3:00 Close

AURIXA is scoped for depth: one integrated workflow with measurable automation, guardrails, and traceability, aligned to AI-Native News Experience with enterprise-grade agent orchestration.
